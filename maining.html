Instructions

Step 1: Equipment

Choose the appropriate mining hardware. For Debol - GPUs.

Step 2: Wallet Creation

Create a cryptocurrency wallet to store your earned coins. It's important to securely store your private keys as they provide access to your wallet.

Step 3: Launch the Miner

After setting up all the hardware and software, start the miner and begin mining.

Step 4: Withdraw Earned Coins

Once you've accumulated a sufficient amount of coins, use the option to withdraw them to your cryptocurrency wallet.

Step 5: Security

Ensure the security of your assets by employing reliable storage methods and protecting your private keys.
